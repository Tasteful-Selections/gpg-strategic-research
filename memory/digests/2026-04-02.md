# Session Digest — 2026-04-02

## What Happened

Major strategy session. Started with Jon's question about how his commercially-focused BI team can become a competitive advantage for GPG's sales team. Evolved into a named initiative (GPG GroundTruth), a full strategic plan, a prototype deliverable, and a contract-compliant data architecture. Pulled context from three repos (strategic-research, bi-strategy, competitive-intelligence) and live data from 4+ MCP sources.

## Decisions Made

- **Named the initiative "GPG GroundTruth — Seed to Shelf Intelligence"**: Jon confirmed the name. Alliteration with GPG, dual meaning (agriculture + data science term), ownable in produce.
- **10-product portfolio across 3 years**: Market Brief, Scorecard, Supply Outlook (Y1) → Category Review, Opportunity Map, Promo Analysis, Basket View (Y2) → Portal, Demand Signal, Category Captain submission (Y3)
- **Two-tier data architecture adopted**: Tier 1 (Claude/open AI — public + internal data) and Tier 2 (Fabric + authorized GenAI only — Nielsen/Circana). Driven by Nielsen contract GenAI restrictions (Section 4(vi) and Data Warehouse Section 2(f)).
- **Portal cannot show raw Nielsen data**: Contract Section 2(e) prohibits "external facing application." Portal limited to Tier 1 data. Competitive intelligence stays in human-delivered products (decks/PDFs). Actually reinforces the right design — matches AvoIntel's model.
- **Market Brief went through 3 versions**: V1 (market analyst — too much pricing ammunition), V2 (sales pitch — too one-directional), V3 (balanced category update — recurring, email/portal-worthy, full product mix, weather, consumer spotlight)
- **Karen White alignment deprioritized**: Jon has escalation path to Tim (Karen's boss) if needed. Removed from critical path.

## Key Discoveries

- **No commodity vegetable grower has built a branded buyer-facing intelligence platform.** Mission Produce (AvoIntel, avocados) and PepsiCo (pepviz) are the models, but commodity vegetables are greenfield. First-mover advantage is real.
- **Nielsen GenAI restriction has a built-in escape hatch.** The "GenAI Limited Authorization Exhibit" means Nielsen expects customers to request AI authorization. It's a gatekeeping mechanism, not a blanket prohibition. Request it.
- **Nielsen Section 2(e) "external facing application" restriction** is a bigger constraint for GroundTruth Portal than the GenAI clause. Shapes the entire product delivery model.
- **CI + MCP + Internal pipeline is the real moat.** The intelligence collection layer (CI persona) + quantitative backbone (MCP data) + proprietary data (Fabric/internal) creates a capability no competitor can quickly replicate. Each layer makes the others more valuable.
- **Market Brief V3 lesson: a sales tool ≠ a market report ≠ a sales pitch.** The right product is a trusted recurring update the buyer wants to receive — it needs the full mix, short-term dynamics, weather, and actionable context. Not just "buy more carrots."
